FAQ

Answers to frequently asked questions about Montage.

How do I get started with the Montage framework?

Please read the Quick Start.

Do you have release notes or changelogs for the project?

Yes! Please have a look at our archived release notes, which include changelogs and known issues.

Can I access the device from a Montage app?

You will be able to access a device when these capabilities are added to a browser. We are actively watching the browser community for such additions.

How can I file a bug or provide feedback?

Please file bugs in the Montage project on GitHub.

More help?

Can't find what you are looking for? Get in touch, we're more than happy in helping answer your questions.

Have feedback or ideas? Let us know by creating a new issue, join us in IRC or post questions to our Google Group. We're also on Twitter and Google+.
